About the Game

You are the last and sole line of defense against the never-ending horde of zombies that are coming your way. Armed with only a pistol, your task is to hold the incoming waves of zombies as long as possible while others evacuate.

However, as you begin to successfully kill more and more zombies, the stakes will only get harder and harder until you are inevitably overwhelmed...

How to Play:

Keys A / D: Move Left and Right

Mouse: Aim & Shoot (Left Click)
